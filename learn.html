Learning Guide

How to use this study guide:

This page teaches you about media pillars and fact-checking checklists:

  1. 1. Role of Media: Click on each of the roles on the right (such as Watchdog, Informer, Public Forum, Gatekeeper, or Mobilizer) to open real-world examples and read details in the case study console.
  2. 2. Literacy Toolkit: Click on each item in the checklist to expand its guidelines, spotting tips, and critical verification methods.
  3. 3. Test Yourself: Once you have read both sections, click the button at the bottom of the page to launch the interactive simulation game.

Goal: Learn how to keep our democracy healthy by stopping the spread of fake news and rumors!
