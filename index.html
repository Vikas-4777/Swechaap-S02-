Portal Guide

Welcome to the Media & Democracy Simulator!

This interactive portal consists of two primary educational routes:

  1. 1. Learn Media Roles: Click this card to study the core roles of media in a democracy (such as Watchdog, Informer, and Public Forum) and the 3 investigative skills in the Literacy Toolkit.
  2. 2. Play Simulation Game: Click this card to face the news feed challenge. Lock in your choice to witness outcomes, review choice stats, and monitor community health.

Goal: Learn how to keep our democracy healthy by stopping the spread of fake news and rumors!
