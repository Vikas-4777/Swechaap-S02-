Simulator Guide

How to play the simulation:

  1. 1. Play 5 Random Challenges: A random set of 5 headlines from a pool of 25 is generated. You will evaluate them one by one.
  2. 2. Inspect the Post & Hint: Check the headline, URL, and click "Hint" to run a background domain lookup.
  3. 3. Choose and Lock: Select A, B, C, or D and lock your choice. The dashboard on the right will reveal the outcomes.
  4. 4. Advance & Score: Click "Next Question" to proceed. At the end of the 5th question, unlock your Citizen Report Card!

Goal: Maintain a high Personal Civic Health percentage by fact-checking and reporting fake stories!
